About Me

Hey! I'm Adam.

I recently finished up my Master's in Applied Statistics from Michigan Tech. Most of the projects I've posted here are assignments I completed for courses in that program. I still have a long way to go on my Data Science journey, but I'm constantly trying to learn more. Since graduating, I've completed the Google Data Analytics professional certificate, I've started working through Khan Academy (SQL) and Data Camp (Python) courses, and I've begun constructing this website to host my portfolio of data science projects.

I've been teaching high school math at Dobyns-Bennett High School over the past 11 years. I absolutely love all things mathematical. My favorite number is "e", although I am incredibly fascinated by Tree(3) and Graham's number as well. I love working with youth because they keep me feeling young myself.

Outside of what I do for work, I'm also passionate about a lot of other stuff. I'm a vegan (going on 4 years I think). I love running (although I've had to take several breaks due to terrible knees). I play way too much Hearthstone (made it to top 200 legend on the US server once). I like building things and DIY projects (still have a lot of room for improvement with my woodworking). My wife and I love traveling, although we've had terrible luck lately with actually getting to go anywhere (thanks, COVID!).
